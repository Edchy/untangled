You know what a switch does. **On or off.** That's still the idea.

Here's the only new thing: **connect two switches** so that one's output feeds into the other's input. Now they're not independent. They affect each other.

That's a circuit. And once switches affect each other, they can do something a single switch never could.

They can **make a decision**.
