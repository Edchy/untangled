That was a lot.

And if some of it still feels slippery, **that is completely fine**.
You do not need to hold every gate, wire, and input in your head at once. The important thing is **the bigger picture**:

A computer is **not doing magic**. It is taking **tiny yes/no questions**, wiring them together, and using the final answer as the result.

That is **the idea worth keeping**.
