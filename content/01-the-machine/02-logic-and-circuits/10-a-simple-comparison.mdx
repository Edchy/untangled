So, is **A bigger than B**?

With single digits there's only one situation where that's true. **A must be 1, and B must be 0.** That's the only winning combination.

So we want the circuit to output **1 when A is 1 and B is 0**, and **0 in every other case**. We want the light on for exactly one combination, and off for everything else.
