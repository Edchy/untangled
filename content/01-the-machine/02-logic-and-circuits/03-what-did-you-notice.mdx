The light did not care which switch you touched first. It cared about the **arrangement**.

Two switches can be wired so **both have to agree**. Or they can be wired so **either one is enough**.

Same parts. Same electricity. **Different logic.**

That is the important move: a computer is not just made of switches. It is made of switches **connected in meaningful patterns**.
