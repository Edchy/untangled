Now you have three switches.
**Eight possible combinations.**
0 through 7.

111 becomes 7.
001 becomes 1.


Each bit you add **doubles** what you can represent.

With 4 bits, what would be the highest number you could represent?

?? 15. 4 bits give you 16 combinations (0 - 15).
