The computer has no idea what any of this means.
Take a photo on your phone. A JPEG.
You see a sunset. A face. A memory.
The computer sees 1s and 0s. Nothing more.

How does it even know it's a photo?
The file is named .jpg. That's a hint, a convention.
But the real clue is inside the file itself.
