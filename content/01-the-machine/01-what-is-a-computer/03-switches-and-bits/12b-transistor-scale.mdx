Your phone has about **15 billion** of them, on a chip the size of your thumbnail.
That chip is made of silicon. A material refined from ordinary sand.
A modern transistor is about 5 nanometers wide.
A human hair is 15,000 times wider.

Let that sink in. It's truly mind-blowing.

**15 billion switches.** Each one smaller than a virus. Flipping on and off billions of times a second.
