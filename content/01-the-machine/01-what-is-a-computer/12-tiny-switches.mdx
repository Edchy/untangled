Remember the switch you clicked at the start?

A transistor is exactly that. A small switch. But made of silicon, with no moving parts.
A tiny electrical signal tells it to let current through.
Remove the signal and it blocks it.

On, or off.

That is all a transistor does.
But there are many of them.
