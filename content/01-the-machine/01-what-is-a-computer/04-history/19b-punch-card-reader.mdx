Here is a punch card, reading the word `HELLO` in binary.

01001000 01000101 01001100 01001100 01001111

Watch one spot on the card pass under the reader.
A hole means **1**.
No hole means **0**.

The machine does not see the word `HELLO`.
It only sees **a line of yes-or-no marks**, one position at a time.

After eight reads, those bits become **one letter**.
