Think about something ordinary. The chair you're sitting on. The screen you're reading this on.

Someone designed it. Someone figured out the material. Someone invented the tools to make the material. Someone discovered the science behind the tools.

**Layer after layer of people**, most of them forgotten, each one solving one small piece.

We're not just using their work.
We're **sitting on it**.
