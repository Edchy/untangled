**The filing cabinet.**

Your photos, files, and music live here permanently.

Much slower than RAM, but it **survives when the power goes off**.

An SSD stores bits in transistors.

An older hard drive stores them as magnetic patterns on a spinning disk.

**Different method. Same bits.**
