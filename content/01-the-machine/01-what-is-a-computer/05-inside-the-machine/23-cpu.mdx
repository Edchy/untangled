Central Processing Unit.
The brain of the computer. The director. 
The virtuoso. 

The main processor. The one that runs your programs, handles your clicks, and manages everything happening on your screen.

It is extremely fast, but it works mostly in sequence.

One task, then the next, then the next. Perfect for most of the work your computer does, but not so good for some things.
