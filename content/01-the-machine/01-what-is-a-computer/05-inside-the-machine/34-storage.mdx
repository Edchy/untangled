Storage is the filing cabinet next to the desk.
Everything that needs to **survive when the power goes off** lives here. Your photos, files, and music. Slower to reach than RAM, but permanent.
An SSD stores bits in transistors, just like the processor.
An older hard drive stores them as magnetic patterns on a spinning disk.
**Different method. Same bits.**
