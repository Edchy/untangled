Think of an ant colony.
**No single ant is smart.** Each one just follows simple rules. Follow the scent. Carry the food.

But together, they build complex structures, find optimal paths, and survive as a system.
No individual understands the whole. The **intelligence emerges from the crowd**.

That's a GPU.
It turned out this made it perfect for **something far beyond graphics**.
You will hear about this again.
