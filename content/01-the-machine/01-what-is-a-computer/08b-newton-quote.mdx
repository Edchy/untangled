The phrase dates back to the 12th century, first attributed to the philosopher Bernard of Chartres and later popularized by Isaac Newton.

__"If I have seen further, it is by standing on the shoulders of giants."__


Newton was acknowledging that his discoveries were built on the work of those who came before him.
