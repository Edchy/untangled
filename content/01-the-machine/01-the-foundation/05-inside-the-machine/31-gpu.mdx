The GPU was built for a different problem.
Everything on your screen is made of tiny dots called pixels.
To draw a picture, the computer has to decide what color **millions of pixels** should be. Many tiny decisions, all at once.

So the GPU took a different approach. Not one powerful processor, but **thousands of simpler ones**, all running at the same time.
