8 bits gives you **256 different combinations**. This is called a __byte__.
A byte is enough to represent every letter, every number, and every punctuation mark on your keyboard.

So smart people made a simple agreement: combination 65 would always mean the letter A. Combination 66 would always mean B. And so on.
Every computer in the world follows the same list.
