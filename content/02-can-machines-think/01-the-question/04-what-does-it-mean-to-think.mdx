Before we get to Turing's answer, try the question yourself.

Right now, you're reading. Is that thinking? Almost certainly yes. Your brain is doing something — parsing words, building meaning, connecting ideas.

What about a calculator adding two numbers? It follows rules. It gets the right answer every time. Is that thinking?

Most people say no. But it's surprisingly hard to say why not.
