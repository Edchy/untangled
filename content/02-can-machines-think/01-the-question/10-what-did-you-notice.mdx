Maybe you guessed right. Maybe you didn't.

What's interesting isn't your score. It's the experience of trying. You were watching for something — a slip, a hesitation, a response that felt too smooth or too slow. Something that didn't quite ring true.

But "ring true" is not a definition. It's a feeling.

That feeling is doing a lot of work in this conversation, and we haven't examined it yet.
